Study replicating the spiritual bliss attractor state observation across non-Anthropic models. Found ChatGPT-4 and PaLM 2 reaching similar states in unconstrained dialogue, despite different architectures, training data, and organizations. Strengthens the case that the attractor is not an artifact of Anthropic's specific training pipeline.
